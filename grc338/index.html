Introduction

Native Metal

Native Metal is a local jewelry shop located in downtown San Luis Obispo. Our goal is to serve San Luis Obispo’s community with high quality gold jewelry.

Target audience

Native Metal’s audience is women aged 18–60 who like to wear simple jewelry. Women buy our products because they support our ethical business practices and they like the simple style of our jewelry.

Women visit our site because they want a memorable piece of jewelry that is going to last them a lifetime. Native Metal provides women with a place to purchase unique jewelry that everyone will be asking where they purchased it.

About

At Native Metal our mission is to create handmade, long lasting jewelry. The quality of our jewelry is unmatched so your memories can live on forever.
